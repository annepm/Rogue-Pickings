The roaming truck bringing you the freshest ingredients and ideas in food!

Rogue Pickings started as a hobby. We really love to cook and wanted to be able to deliver fresh foods to our friends and family around SoCal. We also wanted to give back to our community so every Thursday night we head over to the local food pantry and cook for those in need from 5-8p PST. We also volunteer with several local schools to help grow fresh produce in their gardens.

Powered by lots of fresh ingredients.
